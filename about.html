About author

Hi, my name is Miloš Parezanović, i'm 21 year old student from Belgrade,Serbia, i'm the author of this site which is my first website and also first school project. I'm fairly new to web technologies but i find them very fun and interesting. As you can surly see it's based around video games, i myself have a big passion for video games and video game industry. I've always been interested in their development, and what i learned so far in school was very useful, and i think that web technologies are the great start towards programing and eventually good tools for the near future.

About

Arcade World started as a idea for the school project, and my first website ever. As the name suggests it revolves around old arcade games, which were the first big milestone for video game industry. Here you can find games, developers and publishers who back then established the very fundations for all the video games today. Here you can also join a blog and share your experiences and stories with others, or just relax and learn something new. If you're here because you want to learn something new or because you just feel nostalgic either way welcome and i hope you have a good time.
